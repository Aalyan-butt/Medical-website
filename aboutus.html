About us

1st Care 24/7 Ltd is a leading healthcare staffing and services company providing temporary healthcare workers including locum GP's and Pharmacists, Qualified Nurses and temporary healthcare professionals to the public and private sector.

1st Care 24/7 Ltd aims to provide fully trained and professionally vetted staff within a prompt, streamlined and reliable service that meets your specific needs. Our objective is to provide the best temporary healthcare staffing solutions to our clients, all the time.

Our core values are trust, integrity and passion.
