Nursing & Care Services

Nursing & Care Services:

Whether you're looking for a little help or need more comprehensive assistance, 1st MED has a range of services to suit all needs and budgets.

The quality of our care services depends entirely on the quality of the nurses and carers we place with clients. At 1st MED we have a simple rule when it comes to recruitment: if we wouldn't be happy for a particular Nurse or Carer to look after us or a member of our family, then they are certainly not good enough for you.

Each nurse or care and support worker seeking to join 1st Med goes through at least 15 different checks that include a face to face interview, a DBS check, 2 written references from previous employers, PIN checks for nurses, work permit verification, evidence of recent training, as well as various competency assessments for the role for which they are applying.
