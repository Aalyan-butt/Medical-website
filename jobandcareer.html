Jobs & Career

1st Care 24/7 Ltd. Job Board

1st Care 24/7 Ltd. has positions available across the UK. We need experienced health and social care staff, who have a service user centred approach as the foundation of their value base.

If you have a minimum of 6 months experience in any of the following areas, we would particularly like to hear from you:

  • Care Work - Elderly
  • Qualified Nurses, RGN and RMN for both hospitals and care homes.
  • Support Workers - Learning Disabilities & Mental Health
  • Health Care Workers for care homes and hospitals.
  • Locum GP's
  • Qualified Social Workers

If you have a minimum of 6 months related experience call our dedicated recruitment team during office hours on 01772 914133 or call 07554 770051 - for an application pack to be posted or download an application form from the "document library" section of the 1st Care 24/7 Ltd. website. Please note all job offers are subject to an enhanced DBS check.

For full details please see our selection of current vacancies below.
