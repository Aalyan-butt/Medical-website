On-Line Training

This is the compulsory training course designed to meet annual mandatory update requirements for health care professionals. This is designed to cover the essential requirements of Skills for Care whilst covering their Care Quality Commission CQC requirements.

1st Med Online Course Content/Training Modules:


  • Health & Safety at Work
  • Moving and Handling
  • First Response
  • Basic Life Support
  • SBAR
  • Equality, Diversity and Human Rights
  • Protection of Vulnerable Persons / Mental Capacity Act
  • Protection of Vulnerable Adults
  • Safeguarding Children (Level 2)
  • Complaints
  • Conflict Resolution
  • Information Governance / Caldicott Principles
