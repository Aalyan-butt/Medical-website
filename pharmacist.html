Locum GP's and Pharmacists

PHARMACIST DETAILS:

1st Med has a specialist medical recruitment division dedicated to the recruitment of locum GP's for nationwide clients.

Why 1st Care 24/7 Ltd ?

We're committed to providing our doctors with the best options. We invest time to build solid relationships with both our clients and our candidates who know we're not just in it for the short term.

1st Med is able to recruit and provide opportunities to access thousands of jobs across the UK. We are able to place doctors for permanent roles across any grade or specialty, whilst for locum positions we focus on the following specialties:

Specialties:

  • A&E
  • Anaesthetics
  • Medicine
  • Paediatrics
  • Surgery

1st Med has separate teams for each specialty to undertake a needs analysis to ensure we provide our doctors with the highest-quality service.

Our benefits include:

  • Access to jobs across the UK through major NHS frameworks and relationships with private clients
  • Competitive pay rates
  • Experienced team dedicated to your specialty
  • Full compliance and revalidation support to take the pain away
  • Total service and support for international doctors looking to move and work in the UK
  • Thousands of opportunities in locum and permanent positions
  • A dedicated compliance division to help with revalidation etc.
